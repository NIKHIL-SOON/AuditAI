# Go-To-Market (GTM) Strategy

## Exact Target User
**Title**: VP of Engineering / CTO / Engineering Manager 
**Company Stage**: Seed to Series B ($1M–$15M in funding), 10–100 employees.
At this stage, they have enough employees that rogue SaaS spend is accumulating, but they likely do not have a dedicated procurement or FinOps person managing software licenses.

## Trigger Moment
Right after they review their monthly AWS bill or when finance asks them "why is our OpenAI bill $4,000 this month?". They might be scrolling X or Hacker News looking at "state of AI tools" discussions.

## Where They Hang Out
- **Hacker News**: Show HN is the perfect venue for an engineering-focused tool.
- **Subreddits**: r/SaaS, r/startups, r/devops, r/CTO.
- **Slack Communities**: Y Combinator alumni networks, Rands Leadership Slack, CTO Craft.
- **X Lists**: "Build in public" founders, engineering leadership circles.

## First 100 Users in 30 Days ($0 Paid)
1. **The "Show HN" Launch**: Frame it purely as "I built an open-source calculator to see how much you're overpaying for Cursor/Claude." Engineers love auditing tools, hate marketing.
2. **Cold Emailing via Crunchbase**: Export a list of companies that just raised Seed/Series A. Email the CTO: *"Hey [Name], I built a free tool that scans your AI dev tool stack (Cursor, Copilot, etc.) and finds rightsizing savings. Usually saves ~15%. Thought you might want to run your stack through it."*
3. **Founder Slack Drop**: Distribute within closed founder communities. Offer "If the tool finds >$1,000 in savings, I will personally help you negotiate it down."

## Unfair Distribution Channel
Credex's existing partnerships and credit pools. We can literally say: "Upload your spend. If we find savings, we can provision discounted credits for you right now, directly through Credex." Nobody else has the backend supply of discounted credits to back up the audit's findings instantly.

## Week-1 Traction Expectations
- **Traffic**: 2,500 unique visitors
- **Audits Completed**: 400 (16% conversion rate from landing)
- **Emails Captured**: 120 (30% capture rate on results)
- **High-intent Leads (>$500/mo savings)**: 15
