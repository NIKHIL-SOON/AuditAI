# Go-To-Market Strategy
**Target Audience:** Series A CTOs

## Positioning
Series A CTOs are transitioning from "build at all costs" to "build efficiently." Tool sprawl (buying ChatGPT, Cursor, GitHub Copilot, Claude, etc., for everyone) is a common symptom of rapid scaling. Credex provides a surgical audit to right-size this spend without slowing down engineering velocity.

## Channels
1. **Direct Outreach:** Cold email campaigns targeting newly funded Series A CTOs, emphasizing the "Ghost Seat" problem.
2. **Community Lead Gen:** Distributing the free AI Spend Audit tool in engineering communities (Hacker News, Reddit, specialized Slacks).
3. **Content Marketing:** Publishing anonymized aggregated data on how much money startups waste on redundant AI tools.

## The Hook
"You are likely overspending by 30% on AI developer tools. Run our 2-minute audit to see exactly which seats are redundant and where you can consolidate."

## Sales Motion
1. **Self-Serve Audit:** CTO inputs tool stack.
2. **Instant Value:** Audit engine highlights immediate savings (e.g., >$500/mo).
3. **Consultation Upsell:** High-value leads are prompted to book a Credex Consultation to implement the savings and negotiate enterprise contracts.
