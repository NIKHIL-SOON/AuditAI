# Unit Economics of a Credex Lead

## The Value of an Audit
The AI Spend Audit tool serves as an automated top-of-funnel lead generator for Credex's enterprise services. 

## Lead Qualification
- **Low Value (<$100/mo savings):** "Lean stack." These users receive an automated report and are added to a nurturing sequence. (Cost to serve: Near $0).
- **High Value (>$500/mo savings):** "Enterprise sprawl." These users are immediately prompted to book a consultation.

## Unit Economics
- **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC):** Driven down near zero by the virality and utility of the free audit tool.
- **Conversion Rate:** High-value targets have a clear, quantified ROI ($500+/mo savings) before they even speak to sales, drastically increasing conversion likelihood.
- **Lifetime Value (LTV):** A converted enterprise client yields significant consulting retainers or percentage-of-savings fees. The LTV/CAC ratio is highly favorable due to the automated qualification pipeline.
